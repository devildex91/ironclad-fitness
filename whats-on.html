Whats On

At Ironclad Fitness, we offer a range of classes and activities for all fitness levels. These run on a two weekly schedule and each session lasts for 60 minutes. Check here for confirmation of the week currently running. For bronze and silver members who have gone over their free classes allowance the cost will be automatically debited from the bank account that the membership has been set up from. We cant wait to see you there so let the transformation begin and see you soon, Ironclad Fitness.
